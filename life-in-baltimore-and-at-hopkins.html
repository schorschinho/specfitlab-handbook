5 Life in Baltimore and at Hopkins

The Johns Hopkins Postdoctoral Association advocates for the interests of postdocs at JHMI, organizes a lot of fun events, and is a nice way to meet new people. The Cooley Center has affordable gym memberships and offers intramural sports leagues and classes. Welch Medical Library is your first stop for book loans, literature research, and the meta-analysis tool Covidence.

Sign up for a free library card from the Enoch Pratt Free Library and get access to their amazing physical and digital catalogue. Become a member at Station North Tool Library to borrow power tools and gain access to the open workshop.

Social leagues like Volo Sports are the preferred way to play your favourite sports and meet new people.

Baltimore Banner and Baltimore Beat are two exciting additions to the (alt-)newspaper space in Baltimore. The latest cultural news and events can also be found in Baltimore Magazine, Baltimore Fishbowl, and the baltshowplace tumblr.

Amazing annual festivals in and around Baltimore include Artscape, HONfest, Baltimore Book Festival, Baltimore Restaurant Week, Light City, and many more. Look out for small neighborhood festivals as well.

The best museums include The Walters, the Baltimore Museum of Art, the American Visionary Art Museum, and theReginald F. Lewis Museum of Maryland African American History & Culture.

Excellent neighborhood-owned bookshops worth supporting are Greedy Reads, Charm City Books, the Ivy Bookshop - and you can get books for free every month from the big warehouse at The Book Thing!! (Some recommended books to learn more about the history of Baltimore: The Black Butterfly, Not In My Neighborhood, Baltimore: A Political History)

Movie theatres include the Charles Theatre, the Parkway Theatre (https://mdfilmfest.com/), and the Senator Theatre.

Good day trip getaways into nature: Rocks State Park, Patapsco Valley State Park, Gunpowder Falls State Park, Frederick.
